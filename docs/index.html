Features

Zero config

Works on every page automatically. No watchlists, no polling intervals, no setup wizards.

Smart extraction

Uses Mozilla's Readability engine to focus on real content. Ignores ads, navigation, and timestamps.

Private by design

All data stays in your browser's local storage. No servers, no accounts, no analytics. Nothing leaves your machine.

Meaningful diffs

Word-level change detection that filters trivial updates. Surfaces only changes worth knowing about.

SPA support

Works on modern single-page applications. Detects client-side navigation and re-captures automatically.

Lightweight

150 KB total. No performance impact on your browsing. Smart deduplication keeps storage minimal.

Built for curiosity

Track policy changes

Companies quietly edit their terms, privacy policies, and pricing pages. Now you'll notice.

Follow evolving stories

News articles get edited after publication. Headlines shift, details change. See the evolution.

Monitor competitors

Product pages, feature lists, and job postings change. Stay informed without setting up alerts.

Privacy

What Changed stores all data locally in your browser's IndexedDB. No data is ever transmitted to any server. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Your browsing data stays yours.
